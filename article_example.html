Major Release Dates Confirmed for Q1 2026 including Pathologic 3 and More

The gaming calendar for 2026 is heating up with several major announcements this week. Developers have finally broken silence on some of the most anticipated titles of the year, locking in dates that players have been speculating about for months.

Pathologic 3 has officially been confirmed for a January 9, 2026 release. Ice-Pick Lodge returns to their eerie steppe town setting, promising time-travel mechanics that will allow players to revisit key moments of the outbreak. "This is the most ambitious entry in the series yet," the lead developer stated.

Another big hitter, The Legend of Heroes: Trails Beyond the Horizon, is set to arrive on January 15, 2026. This 20th-anniversary title brings Van Arkride's story to a climax in the Republic of Calvard. JRPG fans can look forward to a refined battle system that seamlessly switches between action and turn-based commands.

Horror fans aren't left out either. Brokenlore: Unfollow drops on January 16th. This surreal first-person horror game tackles the dark side of social media, pitting players against monsters that represent deep-seated insecurities.

Other Notable Confirmations

  • Heartopia - Jan 7, 2026
  • Cozy Caravan - Jan 8, 2026
  • Quarantine Zone: The Last Check - Jan 12, 2026

Stay tuned to G-COUNT for more updates as we track every major release coming your way this year.
