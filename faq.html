Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about G-COUNT and our services.

General

What is G-COUNT?

G-COUNT is a specialized platform designed to track the release dates of upcoming video games across all major platforms including PC, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and Nintendo Switch.

Is G-COUNT free to use?

Yes, G-COUNT is completely free for all users. We are supported by advertisements and sponsored content.

Game Information

Are the release dates accurate?

We work hard to ensure all dates are accurate. Our data is sourced from official publisher announcements, developer press releases, and reputable gaming news outlets. However, game delays are common in the industry, and dates are subject to change without notice.

Why isn't a specific game listed?

We focus on major releases and highly anticipated indie titles. If a game hasn't been announced officially or is very niche, it might not be in our database yet. You can suggest a game by contacting us.
